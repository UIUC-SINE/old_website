Registration

  • Fast and accurate image registration: Applications to on-board satellite imaging (Thesis)

    • Rais
    • Link
    • Great numerical overview/comparison of two-frame methods under different settings
    • Plots comparing runtimes and errors
    • Implementation

  • RASL: Robust Alignment by Sparse and Low-rank Decomposition of Linearly Correlated Images - 2010, 170 citations

    • Yi-Ma
    • assumes aligned images arranged as columns of a matrix will have low rank
      • not a good assumption for linearly translated images (esp first and last images)
    • assumes noise is low magnitude gaussian (section 1C paragraph 2)
      • other models and higher noise levels out of scope of paper

  • Constrained, Globally Optimal, Multiframe Motion Estimation, 50 citations

    • Farsiu, Elad, Milanfar
    • Link
    • sets up multiframe registration as generic argmin of arbitrary cost
    • substitutes lukas-kanade equations to get multiframe optical flow under constant translation
      • doesnt seem feasible at our scale - storing the forward matrix alone is over a billion rows for our problem
      • examples given are small images with few frames - only about 40k rows
      • presentation on matrix-free cvxpy by Steven Boyd may be useful
        • Link

  • Sequential Image Registration for Astronomical Images, 2 citations

    • Shahhosseine, Rezaie, Emamian
    • Link
    • acceleration for registration algorithms, identifies motion direction first

  • Planetary Image Live Stacking via Phase Correlation
